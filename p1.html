隔間輕鋼架系統

Partition Frame System

各式乾式隔間牆面專用，耐用、堅固、施工快速。

Premium steel framing system designed for fast and durable partition construction.

應用範圍 Application

Applications

適用於辦公大樓、百貨公司、醫院、餐廳、工業廠房、娛樂場所、住宅等各類隔間牆。
標準乾式隔間工法使用 U 型下槽與立柱構築牆體。

Suitable for interior walls of office buildings, department stores, hospitals, restaurants, factories, entertainment venues, and residences. Walls are formed using U-tracks and studs.

規格 Specification

Specification

雙側立腳防滑設計，提升施工安全與穩定度。

Dual-side anti-slip flanges improve installation safety and frame stability.
