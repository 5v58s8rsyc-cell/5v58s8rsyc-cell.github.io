隔間輕鋼架系統

Partition Frame System

應用範圍 Application

Applications

各類型隔間牆面適用，如辦公大樓、餐廳、百貨公司、醫院、工業廠房、娛樂場所(KTV)、餐廳、住宅等。
普通乾式隔間工法利用 U 型下槽搭配 2 支立柱組成牆面。

Fit for interior and exterior walls of any building, including office buildings, restaurants, department stores, hospitals, factories, entertainment establishments (e.g., KTV parlors), shops, and residences. A standard drywall partition frame is formed with U tracks combined with studs.

規格 Specification

Specification

兩側立腳邊可加強防滑，防止於施工時滑動；依據板材特性及封版困難度選用不同立腳邊。

Anti-slide points are installed on both flanges to prevent sliding during installation. Different flange types may be selected based on sheet characteristics and sealing difficulty.
