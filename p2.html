Partition Frame System – Accessories & Construction

應用 Application

Application

適用於混凝土施工，因其灌漿程序可節省施工時間與材料。

Suitable for concrete. Due to the grouting procedure, it saves construction time and materials.

特性 Features

Features

● 用於混凝土與壁板施工，具抗壓、抗彎曲、抗剪強度。
● 能支撐牆體，防止坍塌。
● 節省補強與校直作業成本，降低施工風險。
● 符合 ROC 建築技術規則 / ACI 347 / JIS / AISC / CNS 標準。

● Used for construction; holds up concrete and wallboard, and is resistant to compression, bending and shear.
● Supports wall structure and prevents collapse.
● Saves topping and aligning costs while reducing risk of accidents.
● Conforms to ROC Technical Regulations / ACI Committee 347 / JIS / AISC / CNS standards.
