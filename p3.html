T-Bar 輕鋼架

T-Bar System

產品特性 Features

Features

■ 輕鋼石膏天花，目視輕鋼架主十字骨架。
■ 單吊桿 30.35mm 之多樣化設計，符合 CNS 1244 標準之要求。
■ 輕鋼架平面度佳，抗拉強度 210MPA。
■ 塗料烤漆厚度為 20–50 μm。

T-BAR is also named ceiling suspension system, ceiling grid, ceiling tee, ceiling profile in different markets. The easy assembly method of the ceiling suspension system facilitates a fast and convenient way for installation/uninstallation. Its nature of fire-resistance is superior to traditional interior decoration materials of wood. It is available for various kinds of ceiling panel installation, including mineral fiber board, gypsum board, PVC gypsum board, metal ceiling panel, etc.

適用範圍 Application

Applications

辦公室、醫院、教堂，以及各種大型的公共場所。

Office, hospital, church, and all kinds of grand public spaces.
