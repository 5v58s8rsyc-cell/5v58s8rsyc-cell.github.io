T-BAR 輕鋼架系列

T-BAR Ceiling Suspension Series

快速施工、耐燃、防腐蝕，搭配各式天花板材完美安裝。

Fast-installation suspension system with excellent fire and corrosion resistance.

產品特性 Features

Features

■ 輕鋼石膏天花，主副骨架清晰可見。
■ 單吊桿 30.35mm 之多樣化設計，符合 CNS1244 標準。
■ 平整度佳，抗拉強度達 210MPA。
■ 烤漆厚度 20–50 μm。

A modular ceiling suspension system known as T-Bar or Tee-grid. Fast installation, high fire-resistance, and supports a wide range of panels including mineral fiber, gypsum, PVC gypsum, and metal ceilings.

適用範圍 Applications

Applications

適用於辦公室、醫院、教堂與各大型公共建築。

Ideal for offices, hospitals, churches, and various large public areas.
